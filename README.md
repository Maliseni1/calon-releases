## Calon - Your Personal Health Companion


Calon (Welsh for "Heart") is an all-in-one, secure mobile health application designed to empower users in Zambia and beyond to take control of their wellness journey. Built with privacy-by-design, Calon operates offline-first and keeps your data on your device.
